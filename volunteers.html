Volunteering Opportunities

Having the opportunity as a member of UNESCO, the Singapore Youth Association has been involved with both local and overseas missions
ensuring the welfare of the poor families around the world by spreading the message of love and compassion.

what does each Sector of Volunteering do?

Here are a list of explaination of what they does and how they serve a purpose!

    Local Warriors

  • Honey-Bees: the volunteers will deliver food and daily necessities weekly, with those necessities donated by NTUC and ChitZu foundation.

  • Pack-A-Meal: The Organinsation will provide 3 meals daily for elderlys who cannot afford proper meals so that they would not have problems daily,the Honey-Bees will also help to assist and deliver the meals for elderlys who are having inconvinience.

  • Scrubbing-Warriors:Cleanliness plays a very important part for hygiene-care. Therefore, our volunteers will not only help keep the temples clean but also visit elderly residents to help them clean their homes so that they can live healthily!

    Overseas Guardian

  • Build-A-Home: The Buddhist Youth Foundation has came out an effort with go-overseas to join and lend them a helping hand to build homes for the poor families overseas.

  • Gift-A-Love: Buddhism practices an act of giving, where The youth have also spreaded its wings and strive to make the world a better place by donating items to the 3rd world countries which they need it more than us.

  • Humanitarian-Aid: disasters can happen at an unexpected day or time, our volunteers who signed up for this are brave and courageous to partake and help those who are in need.

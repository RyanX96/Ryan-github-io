History Of Buddhist Youth Association

The singapore Buddhist Youth Association was founded in 13th april 1987 by Most Venerable Fa Dun, the chief abbott of Kong Meng Temple. It was a time when he relised that there was many people elderly people living in povety around the neighbourhood near the monestary as they came for complimentary meals provided everyday, he then came up with ideas with the volunteers who are mostly young people who are actively helping out with the food distribution in the temple to cater delivery serive to the households around the neighbourhood. The abbott would encourage devottees to provide financial aid, and also to volunteer giving a little amount of their time to gain some good merits. Hence until today, we have acheived more than 200 active volunteers for our overseas heroes and local guardian helping and contributing to the society spreading love and compassion.
